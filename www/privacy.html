Ture

Privacy Policy

Ture is designed to verify users and help communities reduce unwanted bot activity. This privacy policy explains what information Ture uses and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

Ture may process information required to provide verification features, such as Discord user identifiers, server identifiers, and verification status. We do not collect unnecessary personal information.

How We Use Information

Information processed by Ture is used only to provide, maintain, and improve verification functionality, including identifying whether a user has completed a verification challenge.

Data Storage

We store only the information needed for Ture to operate. Data is not sold, rented, or used for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Ture operates through Discord and may interact with Discord's systems to provide bot functionality. Your use of Discord is also subject to Discord's own privacy policies and terms.

Your Rights

If you would like information about data associated with Ture or would like to request deletion where applicable, contact the Ture maintainers through the project repository.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated as Ture changes. Continued use of Ture after updates means you accept the revised policy.
